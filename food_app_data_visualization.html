School Lunch Survey Results

Introduction

Students from two different classes at Hoover are unsatisified with school lunches offered by the district's food provider, Revolution Foods. Some concerns are that the increased focus on healthy food standards has sacrificed flavor, many of the foods "aren't what students are used to" at home, and the stuents are not able to provide feedback about school lunches. The students proposed to create a survey to collect data on other student's feedback and to show the administration through graphs.
